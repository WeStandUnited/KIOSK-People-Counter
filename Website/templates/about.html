Meet the Team

My name is Robert Netti, I’m 24 and from Auburn, NY. I attend SUNY Oswego as a Computer Science BS major, gradutating Spring of 2021. When I'm not avoiding the plague, I enjoy traveling, hiking, gaming, and photographing my adventures. When I joined this team to work on the Kiosk People Counter, it was my first time working with a team to develop a large piece of software, and also my first time being tasked with implementing any sort of body/facial recognition software. Although the task seemed daunting, the process turned out to be a very fulfilling and educational experience. With the help of my colleague, Billy Bowling, we were able to successfully implement a working body detection algorithm as well as facial tracking and recognition algorithms for identifying known faces. I’d like to give a big thanks to my team for their support and their contributions to this project, as well as a shoutout to our project manager, Prof. Tenbergen, for motivating our success and keeping us on track!
